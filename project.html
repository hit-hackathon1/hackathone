Ear Care Tips

  • Avoid loud noises and use ear protection when necessary.
  • Keep your ears clean, but avoid inserting objects like cotton swabs inside.
  • Use earplugs when swimming to prevent infections.
  • Take breaks from headphone usage and keep the volume low.
  • Get regular ear check-ups, especially if you notice any issues.

Ear Relaxation Exercise

Take a moment to relax your ears with a 30-second break from noise.
